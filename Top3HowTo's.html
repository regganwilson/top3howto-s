How to make a grilled cheese sandwhich

First butter two slices of bread. Then heat up a skillet over medium heat over the stove. After that layer a cheese slice ontop one of the bread slices and place it in the skillet to cook. After a minute or so or until it turns a light brown color, take off the skillet and place the remaining bread onto. Let cool then eat.

How to make your bed

First put the fitted bottom sheet in place. Second spred the top sheet into the matrress and tuck the end of the top sheet into the mattress. After that make corners with the bottom corners of the top sheet thats placed down. Tuck the sides of that top sheet into the sides of the matress. Finally spread a blanket over the top sheet and place pillows,stuffed animals, and etc ontop.

How to tie your shoes

First fold each end of the lace into a single "bunny ear." Next cross the bunny ears so that they form an "X" in the air. After that loop the bottom bunny ear over and through the top bunny ear. Finally pull the bunny ears out to the side away from the shoe.
